WHAT IS NEIGHBORING HEALTH?

Neighboring Health enables communities build social connections through physical activity and commynity functions. From hiking to initiating community gardens, Neighboring Health provides opportunities for communities to increase healthy behaviors. Social connections promote health directly and indirectly. Research has shown that higher levels of perceived social connectedness contributes to the prevention of chronic disease including lower blood pressure rates and better immune responses. In addition, these social connections can create healthy social norms, help people connect with locl services and reach out for resources and ultimately increase health literacy. This is where Neighboring Health comes into play.
