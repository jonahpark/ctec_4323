Better health for our communities

A society in which people live long, healthy lives

Neighboring Health enables communities increase healthy behaviors through physical activity and community functions all while building healthy social connections. From hiking to initiating community gardens, Neighboring Health aims to create safe, healthy and productive neighborhoods and communities across the U.S.
